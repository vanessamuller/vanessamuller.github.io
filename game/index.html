Game Objective

In Princesses Are Falling, you will be tasked with saving as many Princesses as you can from hitting the ground and turning into glittery and colorful dust

Your Mission

You will play as our unnamed hero, who armed with only a heavy-duty basket will try to change the destiny of his kingdom by saving the unlimited number of princesses that rule over it

Unlimited Fun

Immerse into a never-ending-princess-catching adventure in which power-ups and the ever-trustful castle cannon will aid you in getting the Princesse's enemies out of the way and into your basket
